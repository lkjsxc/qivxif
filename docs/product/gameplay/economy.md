# Economy

## Scope

This file owns the high-level direction of the player-crafted economy.

## Canon Facts

qivxif uses a player-crafted economy.

## Supply

- Players craft meaningful gear and trade goods.
- NPC systems may seed basic tools and recovery materials.
- Valuable resources come from world extraction and contested logistics.

## Market Direction

Markets should reward transport, risk, specialization, and base infrastructure.
