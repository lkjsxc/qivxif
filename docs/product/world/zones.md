# Zones

## Sanctuary

- PvP is disabled.
- Hostile pressure is low.
- New players can recover and organize.

## Starter Basins

- PvP is disabled during onboarding.
- Terrain and resources support early survival.
- Travel into the frontier is explicit.

## Frontier

- PvP is open.
- Resource rewards are stronger.
- Claims, defenses, and logistics matter.
- Server authority blocks client-side cheating.
